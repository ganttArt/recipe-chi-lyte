Poha

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Makes 2 servings

ALL INGREDIENTS

1.5 cups Poha
1 tsp Oil - Neutral
1 pinch Asafoetida
0.5 tsp Mustard Seeds
1 Serrano Pepper
0.5 tsp Turmeric Powder
0.5 Onion - Yellow
0.5 Potato - Yukon Gold
0.5 cup Peanuts
5 Curry Leavess
Salt to taste
Lemon Juice to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
Soak in warm water for 5 minutes and then drain
    1.5 cup Poha

Chop
    1 Gold Potato
    .5 Yellow Onion
    1 Serrano Pepper
    .5 cup Peanuts

Boil potato chunks for 4 minutes

Saute momentarily
    .5 tsp mustard seeds
    5 curry leaves
    Pinch of Asafoetida

Saute until onion translucent
    Onion
    Serrano Pepper

Add to saute
    Peanuts
    Potato
    .25 tsp turmeric

Add to saute
    Poha
    .25 tsp turmeric
    Salt, to taste

Cook mixture on low with lid on for 4 minutes

Add Lemon Juice to taste
ABOUT THIS RECIPE
Poha is an Indian breakfast dish. This recipe was made by Vegan Richa and displayed on OneGreenPlanet.
